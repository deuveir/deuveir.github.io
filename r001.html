r001 (originally titled "xinixire") is a collection of ambient pieces and soundscapes meant to compliment the world of Vanea at its time of release, as well as to help put myself to sleep. My vision for the world has since changed, however r001 remains as a capsule of time for when these moods were important to me. You can download the soundtrack here.

VANEAKEU . VOCABULARY

A cheatsheet documenting commonly-used terms of Vaneakeu's universe.

  • Adelos: The "sectors" of Vaneakeu / Shortened alphabetical construction of Areuvel, Desult, Erri, Loire, Ocith, & Soen
  • Crux: The center of Vaneakeu / The inverted "Muln"
  • Dulon: A space which is neither Muln nor Vaneakeu, but an anomaly where the sentience of Tilon is contained
  • Muln: The exterior of Vaneakeu / The inverted "Crux"
  • Mulnic: Devices which utilize Muln
  • Scientists: The avatars of time in Vaneakeu, dedicated to advance each Verse
  • Tilon: A language which can only be understood & communicated by Tilonists
  • Tilonists: The immortals of Vaneakeu / Individuals which can speak in Tilon & exist within the Crux
  • Vanea: The universe including Muln
  • Vaneakeu: Vanea's interior surrounded by Muln
  • Verse: Automated passages of time which describe large-scale shifts in Vaneakeu's technology & Tilonists / There is often only three verses before Vaneakeu resets

AUDIO . OSA

This page serves to archive and detail Osa, an audio project that is focused on composing the events of the synthetic universe Vaneakeu as well as giving a soundtrack to ph418c's animated works. The aesthetic of the music takes inspiration from digital juvenile sounds usually associated with early 2000s Netlables.

this project is now mostly defunct, in regards to sound i am spending more time working on heoliene instead.

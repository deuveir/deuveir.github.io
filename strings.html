2019 08 22 : My Aesthetic Periods / Worldbuildings

2010.04 Soft Virtualization
  • Simulated universe that is from a “primordial future” / archived subjective reality
  • Takes form as an array of servers throughout earth with similarities to monoliths initiating humans
  • Aesthetics derived from my favorite shows and games at the time like the digital world in Code Lyoko or Half Life 2’s Synths, but far more specific in blending artificial and organic designs to a point there is very little that is recognizable in design/environments/creatures/etc.
  • Significant influence in structure from my dreams at the time which were written in journals.

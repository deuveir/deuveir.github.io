GIPNOZA . TOURMALEINE 2018.08

Tourmaleine is the preliminary EP in the Indus series. The release is focused on live play with vocoders, breaks, glitches, and more to evoke a "flashcore"-type sound. Tourmaleine was recorded over the course of a few days in a single project file. It was released with Ploom Labs. You can download Tourmaleine here.

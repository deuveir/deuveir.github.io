2020.12 Signal Juicer Music Video
for my final project, i created a music video for a song i made using samples extracted from my nintendo ds. this video was put together by simply recording my macos desktop while the song played and was visualized by various audioreactive forms i programmed in processing. everything in the video is also written through the lens of my synthetic language, vaneac, which can be seen in all the symbols used throughout the video as well as in the syllables defining each processing sketch.

Otscucora is the first EP in the Sombre series, and is meant to compliment Tourmaleine. Otscucora's atmosphere was based on my experiences walking entire days home in Japan. Each side is a collection of ambient solos using the same tools created for Tourmaleine. It was released exclusively with Ploom Labs as a physical edition. You can now downoad it for free here.

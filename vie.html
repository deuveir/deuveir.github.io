INTERACTIVE . VIE 2015.04

An experimental piece made in collaboration with Moshe Linke. The premise of ViE focuses on a sentient building that "thinks" through abstract deconstructions of architecture. You are tasked to communicate with them, and through your interactions an exploration of space, language, sound, and time. What is geometrically impossible is nature for this building and it only increases in complexity the deeper you find yourself inside it. Some key inspirations were LimasseFive's "NaissanceE", Mark Z. Danielewski's "The House of Leaves", and Tsutomu Nihei's "BLAME!".

OSA . SITUALINE 2018.03

Situaline is the preliminary EP in the Vaneac series. The release was created over the course of a week. You can download Situaline here.

"Situaline occurs during the 1st verse of Vaneakeu, in a newly established Errithasede sector. Each of these tracks act as recordings from an unnamed scientist birthed into this world to document the inevidable future of the region. Every sound was constructed with Ableton's operator. All of the composition and percussion was controlled entirely through midi effects."

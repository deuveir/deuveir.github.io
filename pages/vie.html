INTERACTIVE . VIE 2015.04

An experimental piece made in collaboration with Moshe Linke to create a spiritual sequel for Limassefive's "NaissanceE". The premise was that you, the player, have found a sentient building that thinks through architecture. You are tasked to communicate with them, and through your interactions an exploration of the nature of space and definition comes. Some key inspirations other than NaissanceE were Mark Z. Danielewski's "The House of Leaves" and Tsutomu Nihei's "BLAME!". The project was never completed.

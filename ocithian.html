OSA . OCITHIAN 2017.11

Ocithian is the preliminary EP in the Ocithic series. The release was created over the course of a week. You can download Ocithian here.

"Ocithian occurs in a hypothetical 4th verse preceding the events of Vaneakeu's eventual injection, where the Soen abyss has submerged the entirety of its enclosure. The city is in decay, and its automations are flourishing into beautiful self destructive systems. Every sound was constructed with Ableton's operator, using just a sine wave. All of the composition and percussion was controlled entirely through midi effects."
